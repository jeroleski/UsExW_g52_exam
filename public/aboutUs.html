How to use our service

  1. Before you try to rent a car make sure that you are logged in.
  2. Click on 'Find cars' in the navigation bar menu.
  3. Select a car on the map.
  4. A pop-up menu will appear on the screen giving you an overview of the car. To learn more about the chosen car choose to continue. This will take you to the page where you can reserve the car as well.
  5. Click confirm to reserve the car. Reservation comes with a fee of 50DKK.
  6. When you are next to the car click 'Pick up' at the rental page to unlock the car.
  7. Drive!
  8. If you want to park the car without returning it, click on 'Lock car' to lock the car. While the car is locked you will still be charged 3DKK per minute. To unlock the car again click on 'Unlock car'.
  9. When you are done, park the car and click 'Return car'. Make sure that you have collected all of your belongings beforehand.
  10. You will now receive a receipt and are free to go back to the map and rent a new car.

FAQ

How do I cancel a car reservation?

To cancel a reservation go to the current rental page and click on cancel. The reservation will be cancelled and the initial price will be charged.
